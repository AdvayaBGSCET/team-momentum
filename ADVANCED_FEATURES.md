## 🎯 Feature 3: Future Risk Prediction

### What It Does:

Predicts invasion risk for next 90 days based on:
- Current risk level
- Shipping traffic trends
- Ocean temperature trends

### Prediction Formula:
```javascript
futureRisk = currentRisk + (shippingTrend × 0.5) + (tempTrend × 0.5)
```

### Risk Factors:
- **Shipping Trend**: +10 if traffic > 80, else +5
- **Temperature Trend**: +5 if temp > 29°C, else +2

### Output:
- Current risk score
- Predicted risk in 90 days
- Risk increase amount
- Status (SEVERE/HIGH/MODERATE/LOW)
- Contributing factors breakdown
- Voice alert for high-risk predictions
