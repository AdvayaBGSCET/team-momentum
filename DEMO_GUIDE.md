## 💡 Questions Judges Might Ask

### Q: "Is this real data?"
**A**: "Yes! We integrate OpenWeatherMap for live weather, CPCB for water quality, and our MySQL database has real pollution data from Indian rivers and ports. The shipping routes are based on actual maritime traffic patterns."

### Q: "How accurate is the prediction?"
**A**: "Our prediction model uses a weighted formula combining shipping traffic trends and ocean temperature patterns. It's calibrated based on historical invasion data and can be refined with more training data."

### Q: "Can this scale?"
**A**: "Absolutely. The architecture is built for scale - MySQL for data, Express for APIs, React for UI. We can add more locations, integrate additional data sources, and expand to other countries."

### Q: "What's the impact?"
**A**: "By predicting risks 90 days ahead, authorities can deploy preventive measures before invasive species arrive - saving millions in cleanup costs and protecting marine ecosystems proactively."
